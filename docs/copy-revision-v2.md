## Strategic notes

- Every section acknowledges the organizer's specific burden first
- Promise is concrete reduction in effort, not aesthetic appeal
- Tone: warm and direct — not clever, not salesy
- CTA is consistent throughout: "Set up my free space"
- The product feels like a guide, not a tool

## How it works

**Section heading:**
> From signup to first invite in an afternoon.

**Step 1 — Tell us about your group.**
We set up your Gathr space and send you a login link — usually within one business day.

**Step 2 — Bring your people in.**
Paste your contact list or share a link. Members join and fill in their own details.

**Step 3 — Run everything from one place.**
Events, RSVPs, member directory. No more switching between apps.
